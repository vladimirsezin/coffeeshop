About

Hailing all the way from Mumbai, India, Coffee Covet first started out in June 2015 as a simple coffee stand where one can hear all about the milk produced in Mumbai and its relation to the coffee scene in India. Today, Coffee Covet has evolved into a lifestyle café serving up a full-range menu. From coffee to desserts and savouries, Coffee Covet prides itself on its use of pure uncompromising quality of Indian dairy that is both rich and creamy with a tinge of sweetness.
